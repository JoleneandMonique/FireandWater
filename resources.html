About Fire and Water

Fire and Water is an installation, first exhibited at the Electronic Literature Organization's Media Arts Festival in July 2025, that extends an interactive documentary by Monique Tschofen and Jolene Armstrong called Solastalgia. It's goal is to start a practice of thinking about the twinned problems of wildfires and flooding due to the climage emerency.
